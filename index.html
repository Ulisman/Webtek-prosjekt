Purpose and Goals

Our proposed purpose of the site is the display and propogate the likeness of clients pet animal. We aim to do so through the display of various visual media as well as sharing interesting content that might appeal to dog-interested people. This may be through a human years to dog years calculator, videoes, dietary plan and live age display. These goals also function for the purpose of challenging our own ability to create functionality on website for our client.

Audience

The proposed target audience for this website is everyone who are interseted in dogs. Then you can use this website to learn more about the dog "Nala". The website can both be for close family and friends, or for strangers who has a passion for dogs. If you either want to buy a new dog and want some inspiration, or just want to learn more about the beautiful animal, this website is the perfect one for you. We will especially try to reach the younger generation (teenagers/childrens), because we think they will enjoy this website.
