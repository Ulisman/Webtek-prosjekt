September 20 2021

Our Client

  • Client name: Regina

Our client, Regina Alfnes, is a 19 year old student who just finished high school and is a doglover at heart. Now she wants to spread her love for her dog, Nala, to the rest of the world through a website.

Purpose and Goals

Our proposed purpose of the site is the display and propogate the likeness of clients pet animal. We aim to do so through the display of various visual media as well as sharing interesting content that might appeal to dog-interested people. This may be through a human years to dog years calculator, videoes, dietary plan and live age display. These goals also function for the purpose of challenging our own ability to create functionality on website for our client.

Audience

The proposed target audience for this website is everyone who are interseted in dogs. Then you can use this website to learn more about the dog "Nala". The website can both be for close family and friends, or for strangers who has a passion for dogs. If you either want to buy a new dog and want some inspiration, or just want to learn more about the beautiful animal, this website is the perfect one for you. We will especially try to reach the younger generation (teenagers/childrens), because we think they will enjoy this website.

The content of the site and how it is organized:

There will be a navigationbar at the top of each page with an overview of all the pages, and make it easy for the user to navigate between the pages. Since the website will be responsive, the static navigationbar will convert to a dropdown menu when the user is viewing the webpage in a smaller window or an a phone. There will be a logo at the top of the navigationbar.

The website will include:

  • A home-page with general information (name, breed, habits), and a picture of our clients dog.
  • A page with a picture- and video-gallery of our clients dog.
  • A page with a program that will calculate the age of the pet, and convert this age between human years and dog years.
  • A page with information about the dog breed.
  • A page with the dogs diet plan and food habits.
  • A contact-us page where the user can add their name, email-address and a message in a form.

There will be a footer at the bottom of each page including the logo, the name of the developers of the website, a link to a facebook-group about the dog breed and a link to our contact-us form.

Functional and Non-Functional Requirements:

  • Non-functional/functional?: All the videos on the website should have browser support. This means that visitors with browsers that do not support a certain video format should be served a video with another format instead. The file formats must be supported by the video element.
  • Non-functional: The visual experience should be exciting. There should be a few different different colors on the website, but not too many
  • Functional: The website should have at least one sub-page
  • Functional: The client wanted the website and the url to be the same for all devices. This means we will create a single responsive website instead of having dedicated versions
