The content of the site and how it is organized:

There will be a navigationbar at the top of each page with an overview of all the pages, and make it easy for the user to navigate between the pages. Since the website will be responsive, the static navigationbar will convert to a dropdown menu when the user is viewing the webpage in a smaller window or an a phone. There will be a logo at the top of the navigationbar.

The website will include:

  • A home-page with general information (name, breed, habits), and a picture of our clients dog.
  • A page with a picture- and video-gallery of our clients dog.
  • A page with a program that will calculate the age of the pet, and convert this age between human years and dog years.
  • A page with information about the dog breed.
  • A page with the dogs diet plan and food habits.
  • A contact-us page where the user can add their name, email-address and a message in a form.

There will be a footer at the bottom of each page including the logo, the name of the developers of the website, a link to a facebook-group about the dog breed and a link to our contact-us form.
